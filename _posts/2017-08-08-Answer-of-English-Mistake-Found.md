<font size="7" ><b>英语改错参考答案</b></font>
</center>
#### 一、陕西：      
- 去掉in
- anything 改为something
- better改成best
- decide改成decided
- step 改为steps
- well改成good
- after改成when/while
- a改为the
- just后加to
- enjoy改为enjoying

#### 二、四川
- 加上to
- tell--told
- friend--friends
- sing--singing
- many--much
- so--but
- How--What
- are--were
- him--them
- 去掉with

#### 三、浙江
- side-sides
- from-to
- sit-sitting
- easy-easily
- is-was
- them-it
- not 删除
- in hurry- in a hurry
- felt-feel
- If-Although/Though
